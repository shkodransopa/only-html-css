Dear cyclists of Prishtina 🚴🏻‍♂️, the moment we’ve all been waiting for is here!

Our city is slowly improving its active mobility infrastructure by designating more lanes to biking, like this one in Dardania neighbourhood 😍. As it seems, we’re running out of excuses to keep using cars around the city 🚗, so let's switch from driving to biking whenever possible. Not only is it healthier 💪, but one can also enjoy a pleasant tour 🥰 of our charming city powered by your own two pedals 🚲. So, hop on your bikes and join us for a ride! We’re starting our biking adventure in Dardania. Keep your eyes peeled to see where we'll end up tomorrow 👀.
